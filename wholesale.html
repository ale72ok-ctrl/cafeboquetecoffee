Available Varieties

Three varieties.
All from Finca Gonzalez.

Variety No. 01
Caturra

Clean, bright, classic Boquete character. High acidity, medium body. Excellent for light and light-medium roast profiles. Consistent and reliable.

Variety No. 02
Pacamara

Large bean, bold and complex. Deep fruit and chocolate notes. Rewards careful roasting — exceptional as a single-origin pour over or espresso.

Variety No. 03
Catuai

Smooth and balanced with natural sweetness. Versatile across roast profiles. A crowd-pleasing variety that anchors blends or stands beautifully alone.
