Coffee Cherry Tea

Cascara
Coffee Cherry Tea

Finca Gonzalez · Boquete, Panama

Long before cascara had a name in specialty coffee, our family was brewing it. The sun-dried skin and pulp of the coffee cherry — naturally sweet, fruity, and earthy. A taste of the whole plant, not just the bean.

How to Brew

Hot: Steep 2 tbsp per 8oz water at 190°F for 4 minutes.
Cold: Steep overnight in cold water, 3 tbsp per 12oz. Strain and serve over ice.
